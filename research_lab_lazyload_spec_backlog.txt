  display") had removed single-tab visibility gating entirely - all 7
  Research Lab sections (File Analyzer, Video Finder, Idea Bank, Corpus,
  Beat Log, Workshop, Bibliography) rendered simultaneously. Today's request
  is a real, deliberate REVERSAL of that July 22 decision, not a fresh ask -
  flagged plainly rather than silently overwritten.
- Traced every research sub-module's init(): at least 6 of them
  (refCorpus, beatLog, conidPot's Idea Bank injector, bookworm's
  Bibliography section, contentRepurpose's Workshop button) each fired
  their own inject + a real Supabase fetch on an unconditional early
  setTimeout (900-1800ms after boot) AND/OR on every page:show->research -
  entirely REGARDLESS of which page was actually active, because `.page`
  divs exist in the DOM at all times (just display:none when inactive).
  This means ~6 real widget-builds + Supabase fetches were firing in the
  same ~900-1800ms post-boot window every single login, whether or not
  Research Lab was ever opened - a real, substantial, previously
  undiagnosed contributor to background main-thread + network load,
  landing squarely inside the window Alex reported the 12-23s freeze in.
- Separately found (not part of this ask, but load-bearing for it):
  researchTabs._inject() - the function that actually BUILDS the
  #research-tab-bar DOM element - was never called anywhere in the whole
  codebase. init() only ever called _apply() (which sorts visibility of an
  ALREADY-built bar). The in-page tab bar has never actually existed in the
  live app. Fixed as part of this same pass since Alex's own answer below
  depends on it working.
