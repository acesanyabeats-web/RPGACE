QUESTIONS ASKED + REAL ANSWERS:
Q1: The "expand button" on Chronicles - what should it actually open?
A1: "Full Chronicles log page" - a dedicated panel/page, full all-time
    history, searchable, filterable by type. Not just a bigger inline list.

Q2: How rigorous does the accounting/tax-write-off tracking need to be?
A2: "Personal visibility tracker (recommended)" - rough real numbers for
    Alex's own sense of trends. NOT meant to be filed with HMRC directly -
    real bookkeeping still happens separately at tax time. No audit trail /
    correction history / export requirement.

Q3: Where should real sale/expense records actually live?
A3: "Separate finance ledger (recommended)" - a new dedicated table for real
    transactions (date, item, amount, category, sale vs expense). Phylum 17
    Negotium stays for CONCEPTUAL knowledge (tax rules, business strategy
    insights) - explicitly NOT the same thing as transaction rows, kept
    separate. Negotium itself is OUT OF SCOPE for this build.

Q4: How will sales/spending data actually get entered?
A4: "Manual entry only (recommended for now)" - a simple form: date, item,
    amount, category, sale/expense. No external integration (no BeatStars
    API, no bank import) in this pass.
